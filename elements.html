IT Work

IT PROFESSIONAL: System Engineer



Background knowledge:

Verint is a world renown company in the vast and dynamic technology industry, that specialise in the areas of customer engagement and cyber intelligence.

What kind of work is done by the IT professional?

A system engineer at Verint is responsible for going out to client sites and helping them install the company’s software into their system and further configuring it to ensure the software is running smoothly. In addition, they are required to support their clients in the future if any errors arise or a new feature for the software needs to be installed.

What kinds of people does the IT professional interact with?

The majority of clients for a system engineer are investors. These investors consist of people from different hierarchy ranks amongst different companies.

Project manager:

They are required to install workforce management software for the project managers in order to help them manage their company’s schedule as well as educate them how to efficiently operate the software and troubleshoot any small issues.

Chief technology officer:

They are invited to a corporate sale pitch where they have to display a presentation to sell the “Verint” software to other companies and showcase the strong aspects of the software and how it greatly improve your company’s management systems and security.

Other system engineers from other companies:

They are required to interact with other system engineers within other companies in order to teach them how to troubleshoot the “Verint” software if any serious problems occur in the future.

Database administrator:

After the installation of the “Verint” software, they have teach to the administrator how manage and keep their data secure as the software requires further configuration from their own company.

Business users:

After the installation of the workforce management software, they need to train users of the business how to efficiently run the program.

Call recording companies:

They are required to install the “Verint” call recording software and teach them how to correctly manage the system.

Where does the IT professional spend most of their time?

A system engineer mainly spends most of their time at client sites, helping them install and configure the “Verint” software as well as educating the users of the software how to effective operate the system. Other times if any minor error occurs, they can work from home or at the office by having remote access of the software through the company’s servers as everything can be monitored digitally. What aspect of their position is most challenging? The most challenging part of been a system engineer at Verint is learning a new product or feature for the software in a short period of time. For instance, a new feature for the software came out today and you are required to go the client’s site the next day to install and train the users at the company with limited knowledge when you are expected to have full understanding of the new installment. In addition, they are still maintaining and assisting other company’s softwares while learning new information of upcoming software updates or new technological products.
